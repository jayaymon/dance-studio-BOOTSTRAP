Meet our Instructors

Ian Eastwood

Ian Anthony Eastwood (born April 16, 1993) is an actor, dancer, choreographer, director and previous member of the dance group, Mos Wanted Crew, featured on America's Best Dance Crew. He was featured on NBC's new TV show World of Dance as Ian Eastwood and the Young Lions. He has also worked with The Millennium Dance Complex, Coastal Dance Rage, and Movement Lifestyle. He appeared on Dance Showdown and Return of the Superstars. He has also starred in the 2016 movie High Strung.

Jillian Meyers

Jillian Meyers grew up dancing in Portland, Oregon. There she trained in all styles of dance with teachers Erin Lee, Michelle Mckinney, Candalee Wrede, Diana Redding, and Sarah Riggles. In 2004 she moved to Los Angeles to pursue her aspiration of becoming a professional dancer. During her time there she has become a member of Mark Meismer's company, Evolution. She has also been an assistant to Brian Friedman, Tovaris Wilson, Mia Michaels, Wade Robson, Gil Duldulao, and Mandy Moore on the hit show, So You Think You Can Dance.

Mike Song

Mike Song is a famous Dancer, who was born on August 25, 1986 in United States. He performed in Yomama crew with Victor Kim and Anthony Lee. He has a large presence on YouTube, with more than 250,000 subscribers between his two self-titled channels.
